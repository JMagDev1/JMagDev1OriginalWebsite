When the user starts the program, they will be greeted with this screen asking themto log in. There are placeholders of "username" and "password" to let the user know what data goes where. When the user clicks on the textbox the placeolder will disappear.

This is the users login screen. This picture shows the 2 different ways in which the user can login. Both correct and incorrect. You can see here that the placeholders from before have gone and it is now displaying the username and password that the user has entered.

Once the user logs in they are greeted with this screen. They are then able to go to each of the different sections of the app to do different things like add a pupil or do a quiz etc.

If the user wants to add a pupil, they can press the add pupil button, this will thake them to this page. Then they enter the pupils informationand press the save button, this then saves the pupil to a csv file which can be called from elsewhere in the application.

When the sutudent has done some times on the ski jump, they can add their times from this page. When they press the add times button, it brings them to this page, where from a dropdown box they can select the pupil they want to add times to, once they are finished, they can just press the save times button and it will save the pupils times to their name, which can also called from elsewhere in the application.

The students all complete a quiz before the trip to ensure their safety and to assess them on the rules and proper practices involved with skiing, this score is then saved under their name and can be classes as a pass or a fail.

When the teacher wants to see all of the pupils on the system they can press this button and it will present the pupils name, class and parents name in an organised fashion.

When the teacher wants to see the different levels of the students, they can press this button and it will categorize all of the pupils into different levels(Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced) by taking an average of their times and grouping based on that.

When the teacher wants to see the different quiz scores of the students, they can press this button and every person who has taken the quiz will be presented along with their score, the quiz is out of 8 so 8 out of 8 is full marks.
