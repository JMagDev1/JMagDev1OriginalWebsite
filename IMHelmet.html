A side view of the helmet, here, if you look closely you are able to see some of the different parts of the helmet. Here you are able to see the faceplate and the back part of the helmet. There are around 14 different parts that needed to be printed for the helmet. This includes: 2 for the face, 8 for the main part of the helmet(the part in red) and then on the inside, there are 2 servo motors that have 2 motor arms each.
